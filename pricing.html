Frequently asked questions

What is a credit?

One credit equals one second of video at 720p using LTX 2.3. Higher resolutions or premium models (Wan 2.2, Kling) consume more credits per second — see the table above for exact rates.

Do credits expire?

No. Credits roll over every month and never expire while your plan is active. If you cancel, your remaining balance stays accessible for 90 days.

Can I try before I pay?

Yes — every new account receives 10 free credits on sign-up, no credit card required. That's enough for a 9-second 720p clip to evaluate quality before committing.

Which AI models are available?

Free and Starter plans include LTX 2.3 and Wan 2.2. Pro adds Kling and Seedance. All plans include Real-ESRGAN 4× super-resolution upscaling at no extra cost.

Is there an API?

Yes. The same API that powers the Playground is available to Pro subscribers. API keys and documentation are available in your dashboard once you're on the Pro plan.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. Cancel from your dashboard at any time — you retain access and credits until the end of the current billing period. No cancellation fees, no questions asked.
